## Game description (3 lines, English)

V8 JETFALL is a fast, physics-driven 2D jetpack shooter — boost through open arenas, weave between platforms, and out-gun your rivals with a full arsenal from combat knife to Barrett.
Jump into instant offline bot matches or online multiplayer across Deathmatch and Capture the Flag on dozens of hand-built maps.
Pick a fresh loadout on every spawn, master the momentum, and climb the scoreboard.
